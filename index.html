Tasty Bites Menu

Appetizers

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Papadum

    Thin, crispy lentil flour crackers, served plain or spicy, perfect for scooping chutneys.

  • Vegetable Pakora

    Mix vegetabies (like onion, spinach) coated in a spiced chickpea flour batter and deep-fried until golden and crisp.

  • Buffalo Wings

    Chicken wings coated in a spicy, buttery sauce, served with a cooling dip like blue cheese.

Main Courses

  • Grilled Salmon

    Salmon fillet seasoned and grilled to perfection, served with lemon butter sauce.

  • Arancini

    Fried risoto balls, often filled with cheese or ragu, providing a creamy interio and a crispy exterio.

  • Bruschetta

    Toasted bred rubbed with garlic, topped with fresh diced tomatoes, basil,olive oil and salt, offering a fresh, savory crunch.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Loaded Nachos

    Tortilla chips piled high with melted cheese, jalapenos, beans, salsa, sour cream and guacamole.

  • Spinach-Artichoke-Dip

    A warm, creamy, cheese dip with spinach and artichoke hearts. served with pita chips or bread.

  • Tikka

    Chunks of marinated chicken, lamb or paneer (cheese) spiced with yogurt, ginger, garlic and herbs, then barbecued for smoky flavor.
